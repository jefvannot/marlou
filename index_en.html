Marlou is a unisex brand that was created in 2014.

Its creative studio, based in Paris, envisages a wardrobe to go beyond the usual conventions of fashion. Marlou refuses distinctions according to age or sex and is, in fact, opposed to any and all categorization. Marlou simply presents clothes to anyone who wishes to wear them.

Marlou is street fashion, practical and open fashion promoting a calm and unruffled social logic. It relies on fair production and addresses freed minds. Marlou puts its accent on quality of materials and applications to serve a style that is both elegant and comfortable.

A playful Parisian tone teasing apart the mold of its own heritage.
